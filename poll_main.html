# FBS PBQRating
last updated 12/10/23
### ***Points by Quarter Rating*** * **Methodology**: - Team are awarded 3 points for every game they win and 1 point for every quarter they win in the box score. Overtime is not taken into account (yet), so each game is worth 7 points: 1 for each quarter and 3 for the win. This is deliberate so the winning team always recieves at least 4 of the 7 points, even if they only "win" one quarter. - The ratings are then weighted against recruiting composite scores compiled by 247sports.com to create an average between percieved talent and on-field accomplishments. This effectively weens out the higher-performing G5 teams and gives them a more realistic rating relative to other top teams.
