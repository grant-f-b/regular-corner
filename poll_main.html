# FBS QPRating
last updated 1/12/24
### ***Quarterly-Performance Rating*** **Methodology**: - Teams are awarded 3 points for every game they win and 1 point for every quarter they win in the box score. Overtime is not taken into account (yet), so each game is worth 7 points: 1 for each quarter and 3 for the win. This is deliberate so the winning team always recieves at least 4 of the 7 points, even if they only "win" one quarter.
